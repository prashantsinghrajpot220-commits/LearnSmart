# LearnSmart Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025

---

## 1. Data Collection

LearnSmart collects basic information to provide a personalized learning experience. This includes:

- **Your Name and Email Address** - For account creation and authentication
- **Grade/Class Selection** - To personalize your learning content
- **Learning Progress** - Including XP, streaks, completed lessons, and quizzes
- **Reading History** - Lessons and chapters you have viewed
- **Quiz Completion History** - Scores and achievements
- **Device Identifiers** - Basic device information for analytics and app performance
- **User Preferences** - Avatar selection, theme preference (light/dark mode)

All personal data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. We do not transmit your personal data to any external servers.

---

## 2. Data Usage

Your data is used solely for personalizing your learning journey within LearnSmart. Specifically:

- **Authentication** - To allow you to sign in and access your account
- **Personalization** - To tailor content based on your grade/class selection
- **Progress Tracking** - To show your learning progress, XP, and achievements
- **Gamification** - To provide streaks, badges, and rewards for motivation
- **User Experience** - To remember your preferences (avatar, theme, etc.)

**Important:** We do NOT sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

## 3. Third-Party Services

### 3.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may use device identifiers and cookies to serve ads based on your interests.

- **Data Collected:** Device identifiers (Advertising ID), app usage data
- **Purpose:** To serve personalized advertisements and prevent ad fraud
- **User Control:** You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
- **Privacy Policy:** [Google Ads Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

**Note:** Currently, LearnSmart uses test ad units for development purposes. No real advertisements are displayed.

### 3.2 Amazon Affiliate Program

LearnSmart participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. When you click on book links, cookies may be used to track purchases for commission purposes.

- **Data Collected:** Cookies (handled by Amazon, not LearnSmart)
- **Purpose:** To track affiliate link clicks for commission attribution
- **Impact:** This does not increase the price you pay for products
- **User Control:** You can clear cookies in your browser settings
- **Privacy Policy:** [Amazon Privacy Notice](https://www.amazon.com/privacy)

**Note:** Currently, LearnSmart uses a test affiliate tag. No real commissions are earned.

## 4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

LearnSmart is designed for students. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

**Our COPPA Commitment:**
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent where required
- We do not sell, rent, or share children's personal information with third parties for marketing purposes
- We do not use behavioral advertising targeted at children under 13
- Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's data by contacting us

**Age-Appropriate Design:**
- **Under 12 Mode:** Fun, colorful interface with simplified navigation
- **12+ Mode:** Professional, focused dashboard for older students

If you are a parent and have concerns about your child's data, please contact us at learnsmartofficial24@gmail.com.

## 5. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

- **Local Storage Only:** All personal data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage (AsyncStorage)
- **No Cloud Storage:** We do not use cloud storage services for personal information
- **No Server Transmission:** Personal data is never transmitted to external servers
- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted on your device (platform-dependent)
- **Minimal Collection:** We only collect data necessary for app functionality

When you log out of your account or uninstall the app, all your data is permanently deleted from your device.

### 6.5 Right to Data Portability
